On Wednesday, Farmer Manuel cleaned the farm.

He told the animals that to win the Best Farm Award, the farm has to be clean.

Manuel first put the horse and then the cow into the corral.

He can clean the barn better with big animals in the corral.

Then he put the pig into its pen.

Manuel walked to the pumpkins.

He wanted to be sure that there were no weeds in the pumpkin field.

After checking the pumpkins, Manuel walked back to the barn.

He picked up the hay and carried it to the hayloft.

Oh no! Manuel fell off the hayloft, and the hay fell too.

Manuel will have to rest inside for the next 3 days.
