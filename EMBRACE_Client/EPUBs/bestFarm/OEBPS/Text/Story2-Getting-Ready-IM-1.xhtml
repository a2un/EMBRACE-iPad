On Tuesday, Farmer Manuel helped the animals to look good.

He combed the goat's fur to make it clean and soft.

He carried the milk bucket to the cat.

The cat drank the milk which makes her fur shiny.

Next, Manuel gave an apple to the horse.

Eating an apple cleans the horse's teeth.

Manuel pulled the cart to the pumpkins.

Then, he put a pumpkin in the cart and pulled the cart back to the barn.

Last, Manuel gave the pig the pumpkin to eat to make him fat.

All of the animals were happy.
