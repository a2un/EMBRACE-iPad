Farmer Manuel gathered all of the animals. He opened the door to the cow's pen. The cow walked to the corral. Manuel opened the goat's pen, and the goat walked to the cow. The chicken flew down and sat on the cow's back. Manuel said, "Today is Monday. On Sunday a judge comes to see if we are the best farm. Let's work hard this week to get the prize for best farm! We need to clean up and look good." The cat was so excited that she jumped into Manuel's arms.
