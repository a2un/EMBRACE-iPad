The owl flew to the chicken and whispered in her ear.

The chicken hopped to the pig's pen and opened the gate.

The pig ran to the pumpkins and ate one.

Then the pig took an apple to the horse.

The horse pushed the hay to the cow.

The cow said, "Cat, come and get some milk from the bucket."

The cat ran to the bucket and licked the milk.

The cat was so happy that she jumped up to the chicken's nest and started to purr.

The chicken went back to her nest, listened to the cat, and started to lay eggs.
