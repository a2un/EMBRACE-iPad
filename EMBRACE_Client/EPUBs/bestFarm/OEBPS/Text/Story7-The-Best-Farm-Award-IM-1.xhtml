It was Sunday. The judge walked to the cow.

She tasted the cow's milk from the bucket.

"Mmmm, that's really good milk," she said.

Then the judge counted the chicken's eggs, "One, two, three, four!"

Next the judge walked to the pig.

"You are the fattest pig in the world," she said.

The judge walked to the corral door.

The horse ran as fast as the wind and stopped next to the judge.

The judge said to Farmer Manuel, "You have the best animals and the best farm in the country."

She gave Manuel the beautiful trophy.

All of the animals cheered. They had worked together to become the best farm!
