The chicken said "Cat, cat, come here and purr to me so that I can lay eggs.

I want to be the best animal."

The cat jumped up to the chicken's nest.

The cat said, "I cannot purr to you because I am thirsty."

The horse said, "Goat, goat, come here and bring me an apple.

I want to be the best animal."

But the goat said, "You cannot have my apple because I am hungry, too."

Then, the goat ate the apple.

The cow said to the horse, "Bring me some hay so I can look healthy and strong.

I want to be the best animal."

But the horse ate the hay.

The horse said, "I will eat hay until I get my apple."

So now, all of the animals were sad.
