Manuel wanted to make the animals happy again.

He called his friend, the wise owl.

The owl flew to Manuel.

Manuel asked the owl to find out why the animals were sad.

First, the owl flew to the chicken and said "Why are you sad?"

The chicken flew to her nest and counted her eggs.

The chicken said, "I cannot be the best animal with only one egg."

Then the owl flew to the pig and said, "Why are you sad?"

The pig said, "I cannot be the best pig without eating pumpkins.

But, I am locked in my pen and cannot get to the pumpkins."

The wise owl flew to Manuel and said, "I have an idea."
