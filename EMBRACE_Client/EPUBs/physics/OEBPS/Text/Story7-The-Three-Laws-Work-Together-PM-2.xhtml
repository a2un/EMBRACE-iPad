The force of gravity pulls the skateboard down the hill faster and faster (Law of Acceleration).

Oh no, a toy car moves onto the road.

The toy car stops the skateboard, but Maria keeps going.

Maria keeps going because objects in motion stay in motion (First Law).

Maria stops when friction from the ground adds a force to stop her.

The First Law explains why it is important to wear a seatbelt in the car.

When the car stops, you keep moving just like Maria.

The seatbelt adds a force to stop you and keep you safe.
