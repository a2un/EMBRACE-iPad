Now, push the rocket up.

The force changes the rocket’s direction.

Now, push the rocket down.

The new force changes the rocket’s direction.

Push the rocket backwards.

The new force slows down the rocket.

A new force can speed up a rocket, slow down a rocket, or change the direction.

And, if the rocket is stopped, it stays still until another force is added.

Here is the complete First law.

Moving objects keep moving in the same direction and at the same speed until a force is added, and stopped objects remain still until a force is added.
