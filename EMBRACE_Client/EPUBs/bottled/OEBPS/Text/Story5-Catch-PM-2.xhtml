Lucas asked Olivia what she was holding, but she replied, “Nothing!” and she threw the bottle behind the bush to hide it.

“I don’t want to take your wish, Olivia. Can we just be friends?” asked the boy while picking up the bottle and handing it to her.

“I would love to be friends!” she replied. “I don’t need a wish anymore.”

“Catch!” Lucas yelled as he threw her the ball.

From that day forward, the two friends were always together.

By treating others with kindness and finding joy within himself, Lucas got exactly what he needed: someone to catch the ball.
