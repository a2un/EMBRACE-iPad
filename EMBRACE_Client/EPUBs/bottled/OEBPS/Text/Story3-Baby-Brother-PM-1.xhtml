Baby Brother

Lucas felt silly for making a wish too quickly.

He decided to think harder about his next one.

All of this thinking made him sleepy, so he took a nap.

When Lucas woke up, he knew exactly what to wish for!

He jumped out of bed and ran to tell Papa.

"Papa, Papa, I want a little brother!" shouted Lucas.

Papa told the boy to sit and said, "Son, I have something very important to tell you: soon, you will have an adopted baby brother!"

Filled with excitement, Lucas ran circles around the room.
