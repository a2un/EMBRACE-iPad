Words of Wisdom

"Follow me," said Papa as he picked up the baby and walked down the hill.

"See the river," he continued, "its happiness does not come from things or wishes. Instead, its joy comes from within."

Lucas understood and tossed the bottle and stone back into the water.

"I do not need my last wish to be happy," he thought.

Papa filled a cup with water from the river and told the boy one last thing, "By sharing its water, the river helps others, and that brings it joy."

The boy gave Papa a hug and said, "Thank you for your words of wisdom. I am so lucky to have you!"

Lucas picked up his brother and went back to the house to play.
