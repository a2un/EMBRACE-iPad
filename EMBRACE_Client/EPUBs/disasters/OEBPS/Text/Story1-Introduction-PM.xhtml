Introduction to Natural Disasters

Nature can be calm, beautiful, and peaceful, like a forest, a flower, or a sunset.

But sometimes, nature can be violent because it can produce natural disasters that kill many people.

In these chapters, you will learn about disasters caused by the earth moving and by strong winds.

The earth moving causes earthquakes, tsunamis, and volcanoes.

Strong winds can cause tornados which destroy buildings.

Tornados can also kill people.
